Our services will allow you to sponsor a homeless person and uplift their life.

It can be specific in the province or worldwide. If you have someone like a family you would like to sponsor to help them we will provide them our services for a price to pay. It's a way of reconnecting and making sure your family is safe. We also provide co-op services to people who show interest in the issue and want to get out into the world and help us with our cause. Some people who show interest are people who want to work in psychology or social work and want to get out in the world to gain hands-on experience. Some people have family members who are homeless so this connects personally to them as we also give a volunteer opportunity to anyone who wants to help in this organization. However, we hope that regardless of personal connections, that you may help our members build a life and live safely and happily.
